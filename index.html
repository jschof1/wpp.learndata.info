Data for Creative Transformation: Data Fundamentals

At WPP we want data to motivate, change and grow our creativity, fuelled by our talent’s knowledge and curiosity. We do this by challenging ourselves to think more creatively about what data is available and how it can be used to deliver extraordinary insights for our clients.

These four modules have been developed to help everyone understand where data fits in the WPP landscape. They examine how data is already delivering creative transformation, and how we can work with data responsibly and in ways that our clients can trust.

Each interactive module has been designed to be easy to read through in around 15 minutes. Questions are used purely as learning aids and do not form part of any assessment process. 

Incomplete The data landscape
The data landscape

Data is moving from being scarce and difficult to process to being abundant and easy to use.

However, to realise the full benefits that data can bring, businesses must understand the ‘data landscape’.

This module explores the data landscape at WPP and how we are looking to unlock the full potential of data in creative transformation.  

Incomplete Using data for creative transformation
Using data for creative transformation

Our clients pay for high-quality data products and services that deliver value: value that is created by releasing data from silos and putting it to work.

This module looks at how data provides new perspectives on markets, consumers and brands. We consider how these new perspectives get results when they shape creative ideas, make our work more efficient, and deliver better results for our clients. 

Incomplete Being trusted with data
Being trusted with data

We believe that clients should be able to rely on our data expertise to deliver creative transformation because they trust us: to be responsible with data; to hold ourselves accountable for the data-driven decisions we are making; and to do our best to protect our clients. 

This module looks at what practices need to be in place to ensure we are trusted to be responsible with data.

Incomplete Working with data
Working with data

Data is a creative material. It does not exist simply to be processed in a spreadsheet and turned into a chart. Data can tell intriguing stories, create powerful experiences and lead a brand. But for data to create these experiences we need to ensure that working practices around data are robust. 

This module will give a high-level overview of the processes and skills required to work practically with data to ensure high quality, robust results.
